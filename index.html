Google Sheets Setup

Your CRM data lives in Google Sheets — shared, permanent, and accessible by your whole team. Complete the one-time setup below.

1
Go to console.cloud.google.com, create a project, enable the Google Sheets API, and create an API Key (under Credentials). Restrict it to the Sheets API.
2
Also under Credentials, create an OAuth 2.0 Client ID (type: Web Application). Add as an authorised JavaScript origin.
3
Make sure your Google Sheet is shared with everyone who needs access (share link → Anyone with the link can edit, or share specific Google accounts).
Found between /d/ and /edit in your Sheet URL
